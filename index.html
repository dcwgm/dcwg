Disaster Crisis Working Group Meeting

June 13-15, 2024 | Stockholm, Sweden

About Schedule Venue Transportation Attractions

About Event

Welcome to the Disaster Crisis Working Group Meeting in Stockholm! This event brings together experts and stakeholders to discuss disaster management strategies and foster collaboration.

Event Schedule

  • June 13: Opening Ceremony and Keynote Speeches
  • June 14: Workshops and Panel Discussions
  • June 15: Closing Remarks and Social Evening

Venue

The meeting will be held at the Swedish Red Cross Headquarters:

Address: Lindhagensgatan 126, Stockholm, Sweden

Transportation

Here are some ways to get to the venue:

  • From Arlanda Airport: Take the Arlanda Express to Stockholm Central Station, then a short metro or bus ride to the venue.
  • Public Transport: Use the SL Access card for buses, metro, and trains. Visit SL's website for details.

Discover Stockholm

Explore some of Stockholm's most iconic attractions:

  • Gamla Stan: The historic Old Town with cobblestone streets and colorful buildings.
  • Vasa Museum: A 17th-century warship preserved in remarkable condition.
  • ABBA Museum: An interactive experience celebrating the legendary pop group.
  • Stockholm Archipelago: A stunning collection of islands just minutes from the city.
