About the Event

Welcome to the Disaster Crisis Working Group Meeting in Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia! This event brings together experts and stakeholders to discuss disaster management strategies and foster collaboration.

Event Schedule

  • June 13: Opening Ceremony and Keynote Speeches
  • June 14: Workshops and Panel Discussions
  • June 15: Closing Remarks and Social Evening

Venue

The meeting will be held at the Mongolian Red Cross Society building:

Address: CCD Mongolia Office, Suite 306, Karl Marx Avenue, Sukhbaatar District, 1st Khoroo, Ulaanbaatar, Mongolia

Transportation

Getting to and from the venue:

  • From Chinggis Khaan International Airport: Taxi fares range from 100,000 to 150,000 MNT. Pre-booked taxis are recommended.
  • Public Transport: Bus fare is 10,000 MNT for adults and 5,000 MNT for children. Routes include Bus line 1 and Bus line 2.

Discover Ulaanbaatar

Explore some of Ulaanbaatar's most iconic attractions:

  • Gandan Temple: A significant Buddhist site and one of the greatest temples in Mongolia.
  • Parliament House: A striking landmark combining traditional and modern architecture.
  • Zaisan Hill Memorial: Offers panoramic views of the city and commemorates Soviet soldiers who died in World War II.
  • Khustain Nuru National Park: Home to the wild Takhi horse (Przewalski's horse).
  • Chinggis Khaan Museum: Learn about the history of Chinggis Khaan and Mongolian culture.
